  ---
  Recording Tips

  1. Screen Resolution: Record at 1920x1080
  2. Browser: Use Chrome/Edge in full screen, hide bookmarks bar
  3. Theme: Use dark mode (looks more professional)
  4. Speed: Move slowly, let each page fully load before navigating
  5. Music: Add subtle background music (optional)
